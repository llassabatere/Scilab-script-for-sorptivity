******** Description **********

Scilab files for the computations of the paper 
by Lassabatere et al., 2021, 
entitled "Scaling procedure for straightforward computation of sorptivity"
submitted to HESS

******** Folders **********

0_functions: libraries of functions with two files
- 0_general_tool_functions: tool functions
- 1_WRCHCF_func: water retention and hydraulic conduciviy functions + diffusivity

******** Files in the main folder **********

- readme: description of folders and files
- 1_HCWRFs: computation of water retention and hydraulic conductivity functions
- 2_Sorptivity_cp: computation of sorptivity and c_p parameter

@ L. Lassabatere et al. (2021)
